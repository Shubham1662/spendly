Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Your expense records are stored only for as long as your account remains active. You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the platform; these providers process data only on our behalf and are contractually bound to keep it confidential.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to access the data we hold about you, or would like to request deletion of your account, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
